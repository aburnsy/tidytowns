# Repaint bollards at monument area

## Description

The bollards around the monument area have become worn and weathered, detracting from an otherwise well-maintained focal point. Repainting them is a straightforward, low-cost task that creates a noticeably tidier impression. This has been flagged in two consecutive adjudication reports.

## What the adjudicator said

Both the 2024 and 2025 adjudicators noted that the bollards at the monument area were in need of repainting. As a repeated recommendation, addressing this promptly would demonstrate responsiveness to feedback.
