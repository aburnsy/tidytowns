# Integrate UN Sustainable Development Goals into our work

## Description

The UN Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) provide a recognised international framework for community sustainability efforts. Mapping our TidyTowns projects to relevant SDGs, and referencing this alignment in our application, demonstrates that our work connects to broader global goals. The exemplar village of Emly explicitly references the SDGs in their application and was praised by adjudicators for doing so.

## What the adjudicator said

The adjudicator praised Emly's explicit alignment with the UN Sustainable Development Goals, noting that it demonstrated a mature and internationally-aware approach to community development.
