# Install wildlife information panels at 3-4 key spots

## Description

Installing wildlife information panels at three or four key locations around the village would help residents and visitors connect with the natural environment and understand the biodiversity management work underway. The exemplar village of Emly was praised for creating dedicated wildlife areas with information panels that explain what species are present and why the area is managed the way it is.
