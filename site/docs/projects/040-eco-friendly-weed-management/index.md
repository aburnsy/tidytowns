## Description

Moving away from chemical herbicides towards eco-friendly weed management methods reduces environmental impact and demonstrates genuine sustainability practice. The exemplar village of Emly uses their own "secret recipe" eco-friendly weed killer and this approach was noted positively by adjudicators. Developing and documenting our own approach — whether heat treatment, manual removal, or a natural solution — would be a valuable sustainability credential.
