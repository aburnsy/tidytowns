## Description

The school's sensory garden is a valuable educational resource that could be explicitly connected to the Green Schools program. Documenting how the garden supports Green Schools themes and including this in our application costs nothing but strengthens our nature and biodiversity narrative.

## What the adjudicator said

The 2025 adjudicator asked whether the school was using the sensory garden as part of its Green Schools activities, suggesting this connection should be made explicit in the application.
