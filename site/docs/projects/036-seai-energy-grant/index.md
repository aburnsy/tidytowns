# Apply for SEAI community energy master plan grant

## Description

The Sustainable Energy Authority of Ireland (SEAI) offers grants to community groups to develop energy master plans. The exemplar village of Emly successfully secured a €15,000 SEAI grant through this programme. Applying for this funding would support long-term energy efficiency and sustainability projects in the village and demonstrate strategic ambition in the sustainability category.
