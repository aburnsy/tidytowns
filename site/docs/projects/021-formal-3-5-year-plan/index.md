## Description

A formal multi-year plan demonstrates strategic thinking, continuity, and organisational maturity to adjudicators. It shows that the group is not just reacting year to year but is working towards a coherent long-term vision for the village. Creating and submitting a well-structured 3 or 5-year plan is a requirement for higher-tier assessment and is currently an area of weakness in our application.
