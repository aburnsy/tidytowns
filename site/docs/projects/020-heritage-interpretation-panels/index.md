## Description

Installing heritage interpretation panels at the Norman castle, the old forge, the church, and the bog walk entrance would transform these sites into an engaging open-air history trail. Quality interpretation panels tell the story of a place, deepen community pride, and create a memorable experience for visitors. The exemplar village of Emly was praised for exactly this approach.
