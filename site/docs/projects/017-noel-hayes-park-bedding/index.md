# Bedding and climbers at Noel Hayes Park

## Description

Adding bedding plants and climbing plants to Noel Hayes Park would improve the visual appeal of this community space and create a more welcoming environment for residents. Climbers on walls or fences add vertical interest and softness to hard surfaces, while seasonal bedding provides colour through the year.

## What the adjudicator said

The 2025 adjudicator recommended enhancing Noel Hayes Park with additional bedding plants and climbing plants to improve the overall presentation of the residential area.
