# Sensory garden timber maintenance blitz

## Description

The sensory garden's timber pathways and fixtures have deteriorated, with some sections impeding access and damaged features reducing the quality of the experience. A focused maintenance blitz to repair and treat all timber elements will restore the garden to full use and extend the life of the existing infrastructure.

## What the adjudicator said

The 2025 adjudicator noted that some pathway sections in the sensory garden were impeded and that several timber fixtures had been damaged, recommending a maintenance effort to bring the garden back to its intended standard.
