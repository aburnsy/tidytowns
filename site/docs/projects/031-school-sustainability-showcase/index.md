# Expand school area into sustainability showcase

## Description

Expanding the school grounds into a full sustainability showcase — featuring a wormery, composting demonstration, glasshouse, and food growing area — would create an outstanding educational resource. Silvermines' school garden was described by an adjudicator as one of the best they had encountered anywhere. Achieving a similar standard would make the school grounds a flagship element of our TidyTowns entry.
