## Description

The graveyard extension presents an opportunity for sensitive biodiversity-rich planting that respects the contemplative nature of the site while supporting pollinators and native species. The County Biodiversity Officer can provide expert guidance on appropriate species and management approaches before any planting work begins.

## What the adjudicator said

The 2025 adjudicator recommended consulting the County Biodiversity Officer before proceeding with planting schemes for the graveyard extension, to ensure the right species are chosen for the location.
