# Paint vacant/derelict buildings in vibrant colours

## Description

Painting vacant or derelict buildings in carefully chosen vibrant colours can transform a problematic streetscape feature into a visual asset. The exemplar village of Emly painted all their vacant buildings and the adjudicator was highly complimentary about the result. This approach requires engagement with building owners but can be achieved at relatively modest cost and creates an immediate, striking improvement.
