## Description

Installing biodiversity interpretation panels and species identification boards along the bog walk would transform it into an outdoor classroom and nature trail. Visitors could learn about the unique plants, insects, and birds found in the bog habitat. The exemplar village of Silvermines was praised by adjudicators for exactly this type of nature trail signage.

## Best practice & research

The [All-Ireland Pollinator Plan](https://pollinators.ie/) provides guidance on communicating biodiversity management to the public, including how to design effective interpretation materials that help visitors identify and appreciate native species and habitats.
