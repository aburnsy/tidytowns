## Description

A garden competition across the village housing estates encourages residents to take pride in their own front gardens and outdoor spaces. It is a proven way to engage newer residents, build community spirit, and improve the overall residential appearance of the village. Small prizes and public recognition can be highly motivating.

## What the adjudicator said

The 2025 adjudicator recommended running a garden competition across the housing estates as a way to engage residents, particularly newer arrivals, and to improve the general standard of residential frontages throughout the village.
